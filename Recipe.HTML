Recipe #1; Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef

*Image above does not accurately represent the finished product*

  • Time:1h 25mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients | |Quantity
Ground beef 1 Pound
Onion(Chopped) x1
Cloves Garlic(Minced) x4
Small green pepper(diced) x1
(28 ounce) can diced tomatoes x1
(16 ounce) can tomato sauce x1
(6 ounce) can tomato paste x1
teaspoons dried oregano x2
teaspoon of dried basil x2
teaspoon salt x1
teaspoon black pepper 1/2

Step 1:
Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a
large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
Drain grease.

Step 2:
Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan.
Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour,
stirring occasionally.

Recipe #2; Rosemary Ranch Chicken Kabobs

  • Time:1h 25mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients | |Quantity
olive oil 1/2 cup
ranch dressing 1/2 cup
Worcestershire sauce 3 tablespoons
minced fresh rosemary 1 tablespoon
salt 2 teaspoons
lemon juice 1 teaspoon
white vinegar 1 teaspoon
ground black pepper 1/4 teaspoon
white sugar 1 tablespoon
5 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes x5

Step 1:
In a medium bowl, stir together the olive oil, ranch dressing, Worcestershire sauce, rosemary, salt, lemon juice, white vinegar, pepper, and sugar. Let stand for 5 minutes. Place chicken in the bowl, and stir to coat with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Step 2:
Preheat the grill for medium-high heat. Thread chicken onto skewers and discard marinade.

Step 3:
Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill skewers for 8 to 12 minutes, or until the chicken is no longer pink in the center, and the juices run clear.
